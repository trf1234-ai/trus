About Us

Welcome to Trus, a platform born from the expertise and passion of Umara Kumalia and Ali Alkali, both

seasoned architects with a deep-rooted background in the field. Having collaborated with numerous

architectural firms across Nigeria and beyond, we’ve encountered the recurring challenges faced by

real estate agents—ghosting by buyers, low deal completion rates, and persistent payment delays.

Our extensive experience has equipped us with unique insights into these pain points, inspiring us

to create Trus. This platform fosters high-level collaboration, ensuring agents are paid promptly and

deals succeed through a trusted ecosystem. Together, we’re redefining real estate with innovation and integrity.
